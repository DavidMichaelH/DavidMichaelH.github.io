Lower Bound Minimax Testing Risk

Theorem

Consider a hypothesis testing problem given by $$ H_0 : X \sim P $$ $$ H_1 : X \sim Q $$ where \(P\) and \(Q\) are probability measures on \(\mathcal{X}\). The minimax testing risk satisfies, $$ \inf_A [ P(A^c) + Q(A) ] \geq 1-TV(P,Q) .$$

Proof

$$ \inf_A [ P(A^c) + Q(A) ] \geq 1- \sup_A [ P(A) - Q(A) ] $$ $$ \geq 1- \sup_A | P(A) - Q(A) | = 1- TV(P,Q). $$
